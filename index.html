3 Comma Studios was founded 2015, after the world famous SVDictionary took the tech world by storm. For months, there was hardly a single place it's founders Matthew Hui and Zeeshan Javed could go without getting recognized and asked questions about Silicon Valley culture. 


It was at that moment, when the two entrepreneurs decided to create an app studio filled with men and women who harbored ambitions of creating their own 3 Comma companies, even if they themselves were only worth 1 or 2 commas.
